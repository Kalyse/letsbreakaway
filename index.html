Let's Break Away

A new initiative to take back control of our vacation rental industry.

  • Tweet in Support

The vacation rental market has seen sweeping changes in the last few years as individual marketplaces are being created to offer a single booking experience to travellers. These marketplaces do not manage or have knowledge of the inventory but instead consolidate thousands of property manager’s units (or homeowners) into a single platform. There are many changes impacting thousands of managers worldwide and materially affecting their businesses.

For many, it will be a matter of survival in an industry where their entire business operations are controlled and they are further removed from their guests.

As a vacation rental manager, let’s start with this hypothetical question: would you find it easier to operate your business if you had access to all your data at all points in time? It’s an unfair and loaded question to ask because the answer is obvious, but likewise, the industry today is also loaded and we have a few dominant players who are controlling your entire business through restriction of your data.

Your enquiries are no longer your enquiries, your guests are no longer your guests and your bookings do not belong to you. The properties you market are but a grain of sand slowly slipping through an hourglass in an industry controlled by “information silos”, that enforce rules that you must abide by to continue playing in their sandpit.

A walled garden is being built by companies who have a financial interest in keeping you at a distance from your guests and feed you excuses about “phishing”, “security” as to why you cannot receive the email addresses of enquiring guests. These sites prompt bookings to only pay through their “secure” systems and interject their agendas at every possible opportunity., offering you new management tools to ease your life!

As a community of property managers, we are handing the keys to the city that we built from our hard work and our owned or managed properties to a town hall whose only agenda is to tax us and siphon as much revenue as we can possibly afford to pay. For some it has been too late already.


WHO ARE YOU?

  • You are a property manager or homeowner who is feeling isolated from “your” guests.
  • You are seeing higher costs and lower conversions
  • You are worried about the lack of control you have over “your” enquiries
  • You are at the mercy of a handful of dominant marketplaces
  • Your technology is perhaps failing to meet the new market demands
  • You want to control your payments, cash flow and guest expectations.
